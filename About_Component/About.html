WELCOME TO AGROTEC FUMIGATION SERVICES

Agrotec for fumigation services are experts in the areas of fumigation and allied treatments of a wide range commodities and grain. With over 10 years of combined experience in this field we can provide expert knowledge on how to deal with an infestation problem, the best way to eradicate the pest and provide advice on how to prevent reinfestation. Fully trained in all aspects of fumigation work. We believe that preventing pests or any other type of phytosanitary problem from becoming barriers to the development of international trade is today one of the major concerns of exporters not only in Egypt but also around the world.
